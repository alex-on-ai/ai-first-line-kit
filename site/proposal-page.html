AI intake and pre-visit summaries for Vitamed Clinics

Prepared for Vitamed Clinics · Dr. Andrew Cole

What we heard

  • Patient requests arrive through phone, website, text, and Instagram, and the front desk team cannot keep up.
  • After-hours inquiries are getting lost, which means missed demand and inconsistent follow-up.
  • Intake is still partly paper-based, which slows handoffs and creates gaps before the visit.
  • You want AI-assisted request intake and pre-visit patient summaries tied to MedTrack, starting with a pilot in one clinic.

Proposed solution

Channel intake and triage

Capture requests from the current channels into one reviewed intake flow with clear routing rules, after-hours handling, and human approval where needed. The pilot focuses on one clinic so we can prove throughput and handoff quality before wider rollout.

MedTrack-connected patient context

Map how intake data and visit context move into and out of MedTrack through API or export-import paths. Then generate pre-visit summaries from approved inputs so staff and clinicians start with the same structured picture.

Healthcare-grade delivery path

Design the pilot with auditability, release approvals, and HIPAA-aware boundaries from day one. That keeps the prototype useful now and fit for production hardening after the sprint.
